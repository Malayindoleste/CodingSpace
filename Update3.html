KeyStoreLite Update 1.3.0

Now, you can retrieve all integer and boolean values from the array.

int[] integers = user_data.getAllInt(startIndex, endIndex);
boolean[] booleans = user_log.getAllBoolean(startIndex, endIndex);
We also added streaming mode support and performance improvements. The way to use it is still the same, no methods in the keystore.external/internal.local.Store class have been changed.

We also fixed some bugs that appeared during testing (maybe they also occur in your project).
